ARTEN

Meyda Chatbot

This project was my first commercial project I completed in an Internship at Meydan Free Zone. The chatbot is built using Open AI GPT-3.5-turbo model to answer questions customers may have regarding setting up a business in Dubai. My journey as an Author!

I love to read, so I tried my hand at writing! I started this journey when I was fresh out of high school with a rough storyboard and a dream. I jotted down all possible ideas of how i can get figments of my imagination onto paper, create them as living breathing beings through words. I spent about two years in writing and planning, going through countless changes until I was happy with how Riot and Kinza came to be. Today, I am a internationally published author sharing the story of a hobby with the rest of the world!

Connections, BUiD University Magazine

In my final year of university, I had the privilege to write an article for my university yearly magazine, Connections. After mentally pacing back and forth on what I could possible write on, I decided on exploring on creative outlets we use. After all opportunities to unwind in todays age are hard to find and far between. As such, I wrote about how we infuse parts of our soul and how we perceive others in artistic outlets. Characters as mirrors discusses and explores how even great authors like Charles dickens infuses his work with how he sees the world around him.
